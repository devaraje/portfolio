French-pressed vs drip coffee?

Many coffee purists will argue that brewing coffee using a French press is the only
way to brew coffee since it is far more accurate and all of the flavors and oils grounds
are enhanced while the coffee is brewing. There are several factors that give French
presses an advantage over their automatic cousins.

The French press is a great way to make strong and delicious coffee at home!
